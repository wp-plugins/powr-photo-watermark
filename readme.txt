    == Description ==
    POWr Photo Watermark is a mobile-responsive, fully-customizable plugin that you can edit **right in the page**.
    Add the widget to your site, or use the shortcode **[powr-photo-watermark]** to add Photo Watermark to any post.
    <br><br>
    Protect and show the originality of your photos and images. Choose from standard watermarks, or add your own, then customize the size, position and opacity. Add protection to your images by disabling right-click and drag-and-drop.   <br><br>Additionally, resize, add filters, animations, captions, borders and shadows to give your images a fully professional feel. 
    <br><br>
    The basic version of the POWr Photo Watermark is free!
    <br><br>
    POWr plugins can be used an virtually any website, learn more at [www.POWr.io](http://www.powr.io/)
    <br><br>
    Features:
    <br><br>
    * Choose from existing watermark or add your own<br> * Customize watermark size, position, and opacity<br> * Add protection by disabling right click over the image<br> * CSS3 filters including grayscale, sepia, saturation, hue, invert, opacity, brightness, contrast, and blur<br> * Add and style captions<br> * Custom animation controls<br> * Automatic resizing for faster loading<br> * Custom borders for a poloroid effect, or reshape to an oval or circle<br> * Customize all fonts, colors, and backgrounds to fit your brand<br> * Language support - customize text to use any language<br> * Mobile responsive - designed to look great on desktop, tablets, and mobile


    == Installation ==
    To install POWr Photo Watermark
    <br><br>
    1. Upload 'powr-photo-watermark.php' to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
    <br>
    2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
    <br><br>
    To add POWr Photo Watermark as a widget, go to **Appearance->Widgets**, and drag POWr Photo Watermark to where you'd like it to appear.
    <br><br>
    To add Photo Watermark within a post or a page, simply write the shortcode **[powr-photo-watermark label='MY LABEL']** (including the brackets) where you'd like your Photo Watermark to appear. Then visit your page!
    <br><br>
    Want a more detailed tutorial? Visit [How to add Photo Watermark to your Wordpress site](http://www.powr.io/tutorials/how-to-add-photo-watermark-plugin-to-your-wordpress-site)

    = How can I add Photo Watermark to a blog post or page? = Just add the shortcode **[powr-photo-watermark label='MY LABEL']** (including the brackets) in your post where you'd like your Photo Watermark to appear. Then visit your page!

    = How can I add  Photo Watermark to my site multiple times? = Just be sure to specify different label text, eg **[powr-photo-watermark label='MY LABEL 1']**, **[powr-photo-watermark label='MY LABEL 2']** for each time you use the plugin

    = Why do you require me to sign up? = Signing up is what allows us to track which plugins are yours, and allows you to edit them right in your page.

    = Will my users see the settings icon in the corner of the Photo Watermark? = No! You only see the settings icon because you own the plugins. To temporarily hide it, click on your page and then type 'p + down arrow' (the letter p followed by the down arrow). You can show the icon again by typing 'p + up-arrow'.

    = I can't see the settings icon anymore, what happened? = You are just logged out of POWr.io. You can show the icon again by typing 'p + up-arrow'.
